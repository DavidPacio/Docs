I really like the Town Crier idea.   Your description of “An Authenticated Accounting and Real Time Management Feed for Smart Contracts or any app” has promise – not aware that anyone is attempting this.  How would an Authenticated Accounting feed be used within applications and how would this benefit end customers (i.e. what problem would it solve)?  what is an example of such a feed⁄info source today.  What would be the challenge of getting developers and users to adopt it and what would the cost of adoption be?
 
I’m still not clear what a Real Time Management Feed is or how either of these are connected with the details of Information Manager, SEADR or TARI.
 
What would be the equivalent of Town Crier name for an Accounting (Debt Collector⁄Tax man)?
 
So I think the idea of Pacio releasing core accounting⁄reporting⁄data structuring⁄(management?) product is very sexy.  Accounting central for a connected world.  So, Authenticated Accounting for the connected world, or “Accounting for the connected world” (close to the “Accounting for the World” tagline I suggested on 13⁄10).
 
The challenge now is to be able to succinctly describe what exactly is unique about it (preferably without requiring the audience to read an entire book, as in the case of XBRL and TARI).  Has any progress been made on making an inventory of asset which Pacio can draw on as a result of the three decades of sweat!
