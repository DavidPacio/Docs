Today, in 2019, the question of whether or not digital money can actually become a useful and secure part of the world economy is being seriously debated.
 
Amazing Potential
Despite its drawbacks, digital money could completely transform the current economic situation, beginning with convenient cross-border transfers and ending with the payment of employees’ salaries in cryptocurrency. However, existing blockchain platforms are limited by the boundaries of their own ecosystems. And they do not interact with each other, thus losing the potential for global scalability. The solution to this problem already exists: the cross-chain EVEN Network. In addition to the possibilities of the cross-chain unification of blockchains, it provides the integration of all financial operations with different cryptocurrencies simultaneously.
