Upgrade to SmartLikes
Once the system has a certain reach, we upgrade Give.Buzz to SmartLikes.Buzz. SmartLikes is a rating system based on donations. It will be the world's first true rating system that can't be easily faked. It combines the quantitative measure of Social media likes and qualitative measure of Amazon⁄Tripadvisor ratings. Since the rating is based on actual payments, it is too expensive to fake.
SmartLikes is naturally a data goldmine. Pacio will offer the payment⁄SmartLike solution to platforms like tripadvisor⁄booking.com⁄airBnB for a fee of 0,1%. These platforms would profit highly from a true rating system and they can't create it from scratch cheaper.
