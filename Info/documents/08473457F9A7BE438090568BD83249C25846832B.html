Chain is delighted to announce a new service called Sequence.

Sequence: A Secure Ledger Service for Applications
Sequence is a cloud-based ledger service for managing balances in financial and commerce applications like wallet apps, lending platforms, marketplaces, exchanges, and more.
Sequence is a system of record for managing balances. It lets software teams focus on shipping and scaling their product instead of building and maintaining ledger infrastructure.
