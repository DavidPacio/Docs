Additional Services
Additional services will be added progressively following the initial launch of Pacio. The services to be added, or the order of release could be changed, according to member requests. Some proposed services are:
"   Global Digital Identity Support
"   Data Exchange⁄Monetisation
"   Authenticated Accounting and Real Time Feed for Oracles or Apps
"   Event Triggers
"   Governance Support
"   Ricardian Contracts (Internet of Agreements™)
"   Internet of Things (IoT) Interface
"   Artificial Intelligence Interface
