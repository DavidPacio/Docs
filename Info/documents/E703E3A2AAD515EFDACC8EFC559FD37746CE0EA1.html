Devv is the world's fastest and most scalable blockchain.
 
The Devv blockchain has achieved benchmarking results of over 8,000,000 transactions per second, processed on-chain. The benchmarking tests refer to transactions validated within the blocks of a public blockchain. In contrast, the Bitcoin network can process approximately 5 transactions per second and the Visa network is capable of handling more than 24,000 transactions per second.
