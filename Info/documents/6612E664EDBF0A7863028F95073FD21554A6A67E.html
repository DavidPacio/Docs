2018.01.31 2018. The Year for Data Driven Blockchains. https:⁄⁄blog.bigchaindb.com⁄2018-the-year-for-data-driven-blockchains-1b8ce0b5f9
 
Selling enterprise business software to help companies drive further into a dead end of closed blockchain systems isn’t a sustainable business model other than as a consulting or services business.
 
Closed enterprise software for blockchains isn’t going to unlock disruptive innovation. What Now?
 
The answer is public utility networks.
 
Our own experience with IPDB, the interplanetary database gave us a first class experience in how hard it is to build a decentralized community based on traditional structures. In the end, we couldn’t resolve the need for liability insurance with an open public database for all, and we shut it down.
 
This means that if a cryptoeconomic model can be designed that incentivizes data sharing in a sustainable manner, the data hidden in plain sight finally has a place to go. Data can get published, discovered, priced and used. We believe that Ocean can unlock a $4 Trillion data economy within 10 years.
 
So, in 2018 we’re going to tokenize Ocean to see if we can kickstart a new Data Economy, while giving people more power over their data.
 
In short, BigchainDB is pivoting; not technologically, but conceptually. We are abandoning the idea of selling enterprise licenses and subscriptions, like MongoDB does.
 
BigchainDB is re-licensing all our software as Apache
Effective immediately, we are re-licensing all our software under an Apache license (v2). This means that anyone can use it, fork it and make modifications freely.
